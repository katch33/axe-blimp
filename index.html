Axeclub

Dublin's original axe throwing venue right in the heart of the city. Hurling axes is the perfect event for dates, mates, parties, team building and corporate events. With an awesome sound track and a dedicated venue just off Gaelic St, only 10 minutes walk from Connolly Station, our Axepert instructors will help you unleash your inner celtic warrior. There's no greater satisfaction than burying your blade in the target!
